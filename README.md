# 🌟 FuseAI Fellowship – Student Open Source Project

Welcome to our GitHub profile! We are a community of students and fellows working together to explore, build, and contribute to open-source projects in AI in wide range of domains.

---

## 🚀 About Us
The Fusemachines AI Fellowship is a six-month, primarily online program designed to build strong, practical AI capabilities. Led by experienced AI practitioners, the fellowship focuses on applied learning through hands-on work and real-world problem solving.

The program is refreshed each year to reflect how AI is actually used in industry today. Fellows receive a full scholarship, earn a Microdegree™ in Artificial Intelligence, and gain exposure to real AI projects that prepare them for professional opportunities in the global AI ecosystem.

AI Fellows collaborate on projects that solve real-world problems while learning industry practices.

---

## 🤝 How to Get Involved

- Join AI Fellowship when it opens. Visit Fellowship Website: [FuseAI Fellowship](https://fuse.ai)

- Fork and contribute to our repositories.
- Join discussions in Issues and Pull Requests.
- Participate in fellowship Open sessions, workshops and coding challenges.
- Share your learning journey with the community.

---

### ✨ Our Philosophy
We believe in **open collaboration** and **student-driven innovation**. Every line of code, every idea, and every contribution helps us grow as technologists and as a community.
